## INGREDIENTS
* 4 cups frozen unsweetened raspberries
* 1 cup sugar
* 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice

## INSTRUCTIONS
1. Boil raspberries and sugar for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally; stir constantly for 5-8 minutes more. Stir in lemon juice, then cool.
