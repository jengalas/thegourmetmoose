## INGREDIENTS
* 1 cup plain or vanilla Greek yogurt
* 1 tablespoon honey
* 1 teaspoon+ lime zest *or try lemon, orange or grapefruit zest*

## INSTRUCTIONS
1. Mix all ingredients together, chill, and serve over fruit.
